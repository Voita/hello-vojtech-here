My Hobbies

Tunnel Architecture

I love designing and building new tunnel systems in my free time. My latest project is a spiral ventilation system that doubles as a recreational area for young ants.

Pheromone Art

I create abstract patterns using different pheromone combinations. My work has been featured in the Annual Ant Art Exhibition.

Leaf Collection

I'm an avid collector of rare and interesting leaves. My collection includes specimens from over 50 different tree species.

Ant Sports

I participate in competitive leaf-carrying races and tunnel-digging competitions. Last year, I won the Regional Leaf Relay Championship!
